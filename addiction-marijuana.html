Marijuana / Ganja Addiction Awareness

Marijuana (commonly known as Ganja, Weed, or Cannabis) is often misunderstood as a “safe” drug. However, excessive and long-term use can cause Cannabis Use Disorder, which leads to dependence, withdrawal, and severe mental health problems. With rising cases across India, especially in urban areas of Gujarat, there is an urgent need for awareness and effective de-addiction treatment in Gujarat.

The harmful effects of marijuana addiction can be divided into short-term, long-term, and withdrawal impacts:

  • Short-term effects: altered senses, impaired judgment, poor coordination, anxiety, panic attacks, and distorted time perception.
  • Long-term effects: memory loss, lack of motivation, poor academic or job performance, paranoia, depression, weakened immunity, and respiratory issues due to smoking.
  • Withdrawal symptoms: mood swings, irritability, insomnia, restlessness, appetite loss, headaches, and strong cravings.

At Nashamukti Kendra Gujarat, our specialized marijuana de-addiction programs focus on providing medical, psychological, and emotional support to help patients overcome cannabis dependency. We also work closely with families to educate them about marijuana abuse and recovery processes.

Our recovery program for weed/ganja addiction includes:

  • Personalized one-on-one counselling sessions to address marijuana triggers and underlying emotional issues.
  • Scientific presentations and audio-visual sessions that show how cannabis affects the brain, nervous system, and overall health.
  • Group therapy for peer support, where patients share their struggles and learn coping strategies from others in recovery.
  • Detoxification programs supervised by doctors to manage withdrawal safely.
  • Relapse prevention strategies with long-term follow-up support.
  • Yoga, meditation, nutrition plans, and lifestyle therapies to restore physical and mental well-being.

Our ganja de-addiction centers in Gujarat are designed to provide a safe, confidential, and supportive environment where individuals can heal and rebuild their lives. Families have reported positive transformations after enrolling their loved ones in our structured de-addiction in Gujarat programs, which focus on both recovery and reintegration into society.

Recovery from marijuana is not only possible but life-changing. With the right guidance, medical treatment, and community support, patients can regain their confidence, health, and future. Nashamukti Kendra Gujarat is committed to spreading awareness, reducing stigma, and offering holistic treatment solutions for those battling marijuana and other substance addictions.
